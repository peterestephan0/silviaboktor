The Process

Where pigment meets water, and waits

Every piece begins wet — a wash laid down and left to find its own edges. Silvia works in layers, letting color bloom and settle before returning with finer detail, so each painting keeps a trace of the moment it was made rather than erasing it.

About the Artist

Silvia Boktor

Silvia Boktor is a self-taught artist whose work explores the quiet harmony between spirituality, emotion, and nature. Guided by intuition rather than precision, she creates expressive paintings that invite stillness, reflection, and connection, allowing each piece to evolve naturally through layers of color and movement.

Working on both canvas and premium cotton paper, every artwork is thoughtfully created in small collections and completed only when the composition feels balanced and alive. Each original is one of a kind, with available works offered individually while sold pieces remain part of Silvia's evolving artistic journey.
